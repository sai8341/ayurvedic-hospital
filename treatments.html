VAMANAM

Vamanam is a therapeutic procedure in Ayurveda that involves controlled vomiting to eliminate accumulated toxins and mucus from the body. It is a detoxification treatment that helps restore balance and promotes overall well-being. By expelling impurities through vomiting, Vamanam aims to purify the digestive system and improve digestion. This procedure is performed under the guidance of an experienced Ayurvedic practitioner to ensure safety and effectiveness.
